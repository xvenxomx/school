Why is Maple needed?

1) In Today’s day and age, The Kids get exposure to internet at a very tender age of 8-10 years. Since Internet is a river of information, it also has negative elements in it such as
Inappropriate Content, Cyberbullying, Online Predators, Phishing and Scams, Privacy Concerns, Inaccurate Information, Addiction, Identity Theft, Access to Dangerous Activities.
Who assumes the crucial responsibility of monitoring 8–10-year-old children to ensure they are shielded from the perils of the internet? Isn't it the collective duty of both schools and
parents to impart vital lessons about the negative aspects of the internet, especially given that children are being exposed to it at such a tender age?

2) In the crucial stage of 9th and 10th grades, students often find themselves overwhelmed by the daunting task of selecting a career path. Unfortunately, schools often exacerbate this
dilemma by failing to provide adequate exposure to diverse career options. Instead of proactively guiding students through exploration and discovery, schools neglect this
responsibility, leaving students stranded in a sea of uncertainty. By depriving students of the opportunity to explore various fields for even a brief period, schools effectively hinder their
ability to make informed decisions about their future. It's imperative for schools to acknowledge their role in this neglect and take immediate action to rectify it, ensuring that
students receive the guidance and exposure they need to navigate the complexities of career decision-making.

3) Also, it’s disconcerting to note that a significant number of students in class 11th and 12th are found to be lacking in fundamental practical life skills. These include financial literacy,
understanding investments, cultural awareness, sex education, digital literacy, AI-ML,
entrepreneurship, soft skills stocks, Trading, arts and crafts, etc Often, the education system confines a 12th class student to learning solely from textbooks, leaving them unaware of
practical knowledge and real-life happenings
